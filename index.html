We are a Copenhagen-based company specializing in large-scale outdoor cleaning services for festivals, concerts, and public events. With extensive experience in managing high-traffic venues, we focus on comprehensive trash collection, sorting, and area maintenance - before, during, and after events.

Our team is skilled in developing and executing detailed clean-up plans tailored to each event's size and footprint. These plans are designed to meet the requirements of local municipalities and are often submitted as part of permit and approval processes. We work closely with event organizers and city officials to ensure compliance with environmental standards and waste management regulations.

Whether it's rapid-response trash pickup during an event, thorough post-event cleaning, or implementing on-site waste sorting stations, we are committed to delivering clean, safe, and welcoming environments with consistently high satisfaction rates from both clients and local communities.
